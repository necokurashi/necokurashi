Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Itadaki Japan Poster ("the App", "we", "us"), operated by Itadaki Japan, handles information when it is used to publish video content to a connected TikTok account.

1. Scope

The App is an internal automation tool used by Itadaki Japan to schedule and publish travel-related video content about Japan to its own TikTok creator account. It is not offered to the general public and does not collect data from third-party end users.

2. Information We Access

When you authorize the App through TikTok Login, we access only the information necessary to operate the service:

  • Your basic TikTok profile information (open ID, avatar, display name) via the user.info.basic scope, used to confirm the authorized account.
  • Permission to upload and publish videos to your account via the video.upload and video.publish scopes.

We do not access your private messages, followers' data, or browsing activity.

3. How We Use Information

Access tokens and profile identifiers are used solely to authenticate with the TikTok API and to publish the video content that we create. We do not sell, rent, or share this information with any third party.

4. Data Storage & Retention

Access tokens are stored securely and are used only for the duration necessary to publish content. Tokens can be revoked at any time from your TikTok account settings, after which the App can no longer access your account.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share accessed information with third parties, except as required to interact with TikTok's own API in order to provide the posting functionality.

6. Your Rights

You may revoke the App's access at any time through your TikTok settings. To request deletion of any stored authorization data, contact us at the address below.

7. Contact

Itadaki Japan. To make a request or ask a question, please contact us by direct message on our TikTok account @itadaki.japan.
